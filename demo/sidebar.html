Installation

Unbox your CNC-5000 and follow the included quick-start card. Make sure the mounting surface is level to within 0.5 mm before securing the base.

Control Panel Overview

The control panel exposes jog, home, probe, and emergency-stop controls. A 5-inch display shows the active tool, coordinate system, and current program.

First Job

Load a short test program (a 10 mm square pocket is included on the sample USB drive), clamp a piece of aluminum scrap, and verify tool offsets before running at full feed.

Safety Reminder

Always wear eye protection and ensure the enclosure door is latched before starting a program.
